RAPLIBS
DO IT YOURSELF
Step 1
Download Audacity or any other audio software, the original song, and the instrumental of the song. Google "how to rip vocals from songs", or follow this website's tutorial (and watch the accompanying YouTube video if it gets confusing).
Step 2
Fail miserably at step 1 and find already-made acapellas online. You can check out websites such as acapellaz.com, acapellas4u, DatPiff, and/or SoundCloud. I got most of mine from subreddits /r/SongStems and s/r/IsolatedVocals because they had high quality audio clips. However, they had little diversity and quantities of Hip Hop acapellas, hence most of the adlibs on here are Migos...(but Migos is adlib-heavy anyway.)
Step 3
Open an acapella in QuickTime player. Find the section of the song and trim (cmd+T) it. Save it as "adlib-section". When you open "adlib-section" in QuickTime Player, you'll see the wavelength became longer. Keep trimming down to carefully pick out the adlib.
Step 4
Learn some HTML/CSS/JS, code your website, and badabing badaboom, you're done!!!
