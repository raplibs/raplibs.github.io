RAPLIBS
DO IT YOURSELF
Step 1
Download Audacity or any other audio software, the original song, and the instrumental of the song. Google "how to rip vocals from songs", or follow this website's tutorial (and watch the accompanying YouTube video if it gets confusing).
Step 2
Fail miserably at step 1 and find already-made acapellas online. You can check out websites such as acapellaz.com, acapellas4u, DatPiff, and/or SoundCloud. I got most of mine from subreddits /r/SongStems and s/r/IsolatedVocals because they had high quality audio clips. However, the selection of Hip Hop acapellas was limited, hence most of the adlibs on here are Migos...(but Migos is adlib-heavy anyway.)
Step 3
Open an acapella in QuickTime player. Find the section of the song that contains the adlib and trim (cmd+T) it. Save it as "adlib-section". When you open "adlib-section" in QuickTime Player, you'll see that the wavelength of the audio clip is longer – this allows you to carefully grab the adlib without the picking up the other sounds.
Step 4
Learn some HTML/CSS/JS, code your website, and badabing badaboom, you're done!!!
